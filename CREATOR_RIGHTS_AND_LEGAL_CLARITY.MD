### Section 1 — Using SoundBridge Without Hosting Your Music

State clearly: A creator can use SoundBridge fully 
for professional networking, event promotion, the 
Request Room, fan tipping, and the service marketplace 
without ever uploading a single piece of audio content. 
Hosting music or podcasts on SoundBridge is entirely 
optional, not a requirement to use any other feature.

If a creator already has an existing distribution 
partner handling their releases and royalties, that 
relationship is completely unaffected by joining 
SoundBridge for event promotion and fan engagement alone.

### Section 2 — If You Choose to Also Host Content 
### on SoundBridge

State clearly, in this order:

You retain full and complete ownership of the 
copyright in everything you upload, at all times. 
SoundBridge does not acquire ownership of your music, 
lyrics, recordings or any content you upload, under 
any circumstance.

By uploading, you grant SoundBridge a limited, 
non-exclusive license solely to host, stream and 
display that content on the SoundBridge platform 
for the purpose of making it available to listeners 
and supporting tipping functionality.

Non-exclusive means exactly what it says, you remain 
completely free to have the same content simultaneously 
distributed by any other distributor, label or platform. 
SoundBridge hosting your content does not prevent or 
restrict your existing distribution arrangement in any way.

You may remove your content or close your account at 
any time, which ends SoundBridge's license to that 
content immediately.

### Section 3 — Distribution to Streaming Platforms

SoundBridge separately offers an optional facilitation 
service connecting creators with MBG Sonics for 
distribution to Spotify, Apple Music and other major 
platforms, for a flat one-time fee per release.

State clearly: this is entirely separate from hosting 
on SoundBridge, entirely optional, and entirely 
independent of any existing distribution relationship 
a creator already has. A creator can decline this 
service entirely and keep their current distributor 
for that function while still using every other part 
of SoundBridge.

SoundBridge does not collect, hold or distribute 
streaming royalties on a creator's behalf. Royalty 
arrangements for distributed content are handled 
directly between the creator and MBG Sonics or the 
creator's existing distributor, never through SoundBridge.

### Section 4 — Direct Fan Tips, How Money Moves

Tips paid by fans go directly to the creator via the 
existing Stripe payment infrastructure. The creator 
keeps 85 to 90% depending on subscription tier. 
SoundBridge retains the remaining percentage as a 
platform fee. SoundBridge does not take any ownership 
stake, royalty interest, or ongoing percentage of a 
creator's underlying music rights, only the stated 
percentage of in-app tips and transactions.

### Section 5 — Content Standards Affirmed

State explicitly and clearly, addressing the specific 
concern raised about censorship of religious lyrical 
content: SoundBridge does not censor lyrical content 
on the basis of religious references, including 
references to Jesus or any other religious figure or 
term, provided the content otherwise complies with 
standard platform content guidelines around legality 
and harm. Discovery matching is based on genre, mood 
and location preference, not content moderation of 
religious expression.

### Section 6 — Partner Referral Terms

If applicable, clearly state the 10% referral

to a paid plan), how it is paid, and that it creates 
no ownership or equity relationship of any kind between 
SoundBridge and the referring creator.
