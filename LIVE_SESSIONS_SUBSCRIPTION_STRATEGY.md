- Require phone verification for live sessions
- IP-based rate limiting
- Device fingerprinting
- Manual review for suspicious activity

**Mitigation:**
- Live sessions and uploads are complementary
- Encourage creators to promote uploads in live sessions
- Show data that creators who do both earn more
- Bundle features together (not separate)

**Mitigation:**
- Use battle-tested infrastructure (Agora.io)
- 99.9% uptime SLA for Enterprise
- Real-time monitoring and alerts
- Automatic failover and redundancy
- 24/7 support for Enterprise
